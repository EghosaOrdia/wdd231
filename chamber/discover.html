About Abuja: A Planned City

Abuja, Nigeria's capital since 1991, is a masterpiece of modern urban planning. As a planned city, it was built from the ground up to be the heart of the nation's governance and a symbol of unity. It stands today as a testament to strategic development, hosting key federal institutions, international embassies, and a vibrant, growing community.

Its well-organized infrastructure, lush green spaces, and modern architecture create a unique environment that balances administrative functions with a high quality of life.
